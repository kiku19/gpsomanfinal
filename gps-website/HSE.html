HSE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

GPS Management considers Health, Safety and Environment as the key business concerns in today's World. The right approach by the GPS Management helps us to control risks, cut costs and get a competitive edge. A systematic approach by our HSE Management Team helped the Company to comply with all the requirements and to make sure that everyone involved in our operations, including our Sub-Contractor personnel, knows the right way of taking up their responsibilities towards the Health and Safety of every person and help to create a pleasant environment to work. GPS’s records on Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) Management shows its commitment to the one of the most important aspect of our every-day and working life. Compliance to GPS HSE Standards is one of the pre-employment conditions and this is strictly monitored. GPS’s HSE Management Team consists of over 35 HSE Professionals headed by the Corporate HSE Manager. The Company had bagged several HSE Milestone Awards in the past and our HSE System is rated as one of the best amongst the contracting community in Oman.

BARKHA WORKSHOP

GPS owns a full fledged DSS prefabrication facility of 16,500 Sq.M at Barkha. This facility has successfully completed prefabrication for past four projects of BP Khazzan in record time. At present the DSS prefab and assembly of Wellsite skids are ongoing for BP Wellsite Contract.Initially Precasting will be started in Barka Precast Yard and in the event of non availability of GGBS (Ground Granulated Blast Furnace Slag) concrete at Khazzan GPS intend to set up their own Batching plant at Khazzan.
