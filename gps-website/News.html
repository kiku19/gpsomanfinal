New pipeline construction contracts awarded for major projects in Oman South

GPS confirms that the contract awarded for Tender- OGC-01-2020, EPC for South Grid Debottlenecking Phase 2. This Scope of Work covers the complete Engineering, Procurement, Construction, Testing, Pre-commissioning, Commissioning, Guarantee run and Handing over of the Pipelines, Launcher Receiver and BVS stations and all associated facilities. The following high-level activities are part of the South Grid Debottlenecking Phase 2 project Scope of Work:


· Installation of new 32" loop line (32.2 Km approx.) from existing Pig Receiver area at BVS-09 to BVS-10

· Installation of new 32"' loop line from BYS-17 to Raysut termination point (175.6 Km approx.) on the existing 24" South Grid Gas Pipeline to Salalah including launcher and Receiver traps including the construction of 6 new BYS and Tie-in to existing 24" pipeline.

· New 72 core FOC cable along with 32" loop lines (BYS-9 to BYS-10, BYS-17 to Salalah GSS).

· Power supply, control and telecom system (integrated system) modification for the 32" new loop lines. CP system for the new 32" loop lines
