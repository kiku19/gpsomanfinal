Fabrication and Installation

GPS owns a full fledged DSS prefabrication facility of 16,500 Sq.M at Barkha. This facility has successfully completed prefabrication for past four projects of BP Khazzan in record time. At present the DSS prefab and assembly of Wellsite skids are ongoing for BP Wellsite Contract.Initially Precasting will be started in Barka Precast Yard and in the event of non availability of GGBS (Ground Granulated Blast Furnace Slag) concrete at Khazzan GPS intend to set up their own Batching plant at Khazzan.

FABSHOP FACILITIES

GPS Fabrication facility is at Barka which is ideally located between Sohar & Muscat and near to Express Highway. GPS Facility is well equipped to cater all types of fabrication like process skids, process piping, Vessels, heavy structural, Painting & Testing. Covered Facilities include Office Room with Conference facility, Safety Training Centre, heavy fabrication bays, vessel fabrication unit, High pressure testing bunks, Mechanised blasting units & painting bay, Pre casting area and areas exclusively for exotic materials welding.

Total area of the facility is in excess of 50,000 Sq M with Fully Covered fabrication areas of around 6,000 Sq M. with EOT crane & material handling system. The Facility also includes sufficient Material storage areas (covered & open), Finished Product area, Loading/ unloading area.

Our operations are certified to ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System, ISO/TS 29001, ISO 14001:2004 & OHAS 18001:2007. Facility is approved by prestigious Clients like Petroleum Development Oman, ORPIC, BP in Oman, Oman Gas Company, OOCEP. GPS Fabrication facility also certified and authorized by ASME to use U stamp and NBIC certified to use R stamp for the repair and alteration of boilers, pressure vessels and pressure retaining items.
