About Us

Gulf Petrochemical Services & Trading L.L.C (GPS) is a leading 100% Omani owned Contracting and Trading Company with active participation in Oman's Hydrocarbon, Petrochemical and Energy Sector industries since 1983.

The Company is currently rated as one of the leading EPC Contractors in the Sultanate of Oman with business activities spread across GCC member Countries.

Corporate Information


Year of Establishment  :  1983

Number of Employees  :  Approx. 5000 manpower in its direct employment.

Plant & Equipment  :   A fleet of more than 1750 pieces of plant and equipment.

Offices  :  More than 6000 Sq. M. of Air-conditioned & modern Office facilities in the Capital Area. In addition to this office facility, we have a large office facility available within the Central Facilities of GPS in Barka and Jiffnain All project locations are also equipped with the most modern office facilities.

Office Location  :  The Head Office located at Al Khuwair close to Government Ministries.

Accommodation  :  Accommodation facilities available at various Interior project locations. This is in addition to various accommodation facilities available within the Capital Area including the 1200 Man Camp in our Central Facilities at Barka.

Fabrication Shops  :  Central Fabrication shop and related infrastructure is located within the Central Facilities in Barka. Additionally, a number of fabrication and workshop facilities are available at Interior locations as part of our project site facilities. Total Area of these facilities is approx. 120,000 Sq. M.

Workshops  :   More than 8000 Sq. M. of workshop facilities with necessary tools and instruments are located within the Central Facilities.

Storage  :  Over 8000 Sq. M. of covered storage and 25000 Sq. M. of open storage area is available. Resources and industrial facilities owned and used by the company meet with the highest industry standards in terms of safety, utility and quality.

Annual Turnover  :  GPS is financially strong and have performed positively since the start of its operations. GPS had excellent growth and hold on to an average Annual Turnover of approx. US $ 300 Million during the last 10 years.
