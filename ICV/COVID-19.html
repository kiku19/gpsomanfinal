COVID-19 MANAGEMENT PLAN

Safety is a core priority for GPS. We embrace a safety mindset and build it into how we work. Gulf Petrochemical Services and Trading LLC will adopt the management of COVID-19 prevention as an obligation in its operations to ensure business continuity without affecting its employee’s health and welfare and will focus and implement each action covered in this plan to achieve the below objectives.

People safety
· Coach employees through awareness about the COVID-19.
· Display informative posters across prominent locations to recall employees of personal hygiene.
· Safe work environment without significant risk of COVID-19 possibility.
· Manage suspected cases efficiently with no effects to other employees within GPS operations.
· Get effective and reliable updates on the ongoing COVID-19 and communicate to take suggested actions.
· Quarantine employees suspected to be infected by COVD-19 to safe guard other employees and control the widespread of COVID-19.
· Manage testing, treatment and return to work of employees infected with SARS-Cov2.
· To perform contact tracing and effectively manage the employees that have come in contact with other infected employees.


Our impact does not stop at the site or the office door—we also have initiatives that are contributing to the safety of the people in the communities where we live and work.
