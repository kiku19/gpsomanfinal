In-Country Value

•GPS aims to share the benefits of the oil and gas industry across Oman through its In-Country Value (ICV) strategy. GPS is committed to maximizing the procurement of local goods and services as well as improving the capacity and capability of Omani people and companies in order to secure sustainable commercial benefits for the Sultanate.

Omanization and training

GPS has over 30% of Omanization in our operations. We have been providing adequate training and promotions to our Omani Employees in line with the guidelines and requirements pertaining to our project activities.

In addition to the above, GPS is an OPAL Certified company on its Omanization levels and have a published Omanization Plan and Policy within its operations which is strictly complied with.
We have Omanization process across various levels of contract and operations management apart from the junior staff level.
We are in the process of inducting more Omani as HSE Advisors in addition to number of trained personnel in procurement & purchase, accounts, administration, supervisory personnel in civil, mechanical and electrical disciplines, supply chain management and ICV implementation
